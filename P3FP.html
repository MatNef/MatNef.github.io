Hobbit Meals

If Hobbits lived in modern USA, these would be the meals and restaurants they would enjoy:

1st Breakfast

  • Grand Slam at Dennys.
  • Donuts (They might do Dunkin, but there are plenty of better quality donuts from local donut shops in most cities).
  • Cracker Barrel. No reason necessary, they just would.
  • All you can eat pancakes at IHOP.

2nd Breakfast

  • All you can eat pancakes at IHOP (They would be there from open until 10:30 AM, depends when breakfast at McDonald's ends).
  • Drive Thru at McDonald's (they would get breakfast meals for the hashbrowns, with an extra order of hashbrowns).
  • Scones with jellies and jams.

Elevenses

  • Any French cafe (pastries and mimosas).
  • Hotel coffee / tea (they wouldn't stay at the hotel, they would just go there for coffee and tea).
  • Cake from a bakery inside a grocery shop.

Luncheon

  • Food Truck (any cuisine). Overall, Hobbits dislike adventure but I doubt their palate is limited.
  • Chinese food / Pan Asian (they will have tried everything on the menu six times at this point).
  • Any restaurant with a bar (to enjoy a beer or ale with their meal).

Afternoon Tea

  • Tea, made at home. Probably Twinnings brand.
  • Any baked goods they made at home. Probably a pie or cobbler. Whatever is in season.
  • Cookies. Either something light and fluffy or small and crisp.

Dinner

  • Steak, or any meat, and vegetables from the grill. By the way, they are all King of the Hill-level grillmasters.
  • Thanksgiving style dinner despite not being November. There will definitely be roasted turkey (They put the bird in around afternoon tea).
  • Any Italian restaurant with several orders of breadsticks. Red wine unless they are having a white sauce or fish.

Supper

  • Soup.
  • Salad.
  • Anything light. Just something to hold them over until they wake up for breakfast.
